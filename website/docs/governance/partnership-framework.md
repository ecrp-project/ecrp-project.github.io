# Partnership Framework Agreement (Template)

## Article 1 — Purpose of the Agreement

This Partnership Framework Agreement defines the general principles governing cooperation between the European Civic Resilience Programme (ECRP) and partner institutions.

It serves as a non-exclusive template and may be adapted to specific cooperation contexts, provided that ECRP governance principles are respected.

---

## Article 2 — Nature of Partnership

1. Partnership with ECRP does not constitute endorsement of political positions or policy agendas.  
2. Partners cooperate with ECRP solely for educational and civic purposes consistent with the Programme’s objectives.

---

## Article 3 — Responsibilities of Partners

Partner institutions agree to:

- respect the ECRP Project Charter and governance documents;
- organise activities in accordance with ECRP rules and procedures;
- refrain from using ECRP activities for political, ideological, or commercial promotion;
- ensure participant safety and respectful conduct.

---

## Article 4 — Use of Name and Materials

1. Use of the name “European Civic Resilience Programme” and related materials is permitted only within the scope of agreed activities.  
2. Any public communication referencing ECRP must accurately reflect the nature of the cooperation.

---

## Article 5 — Compliance and Review

1. Partnerships may be reviewed to ensure ongoing compliance with ECRP governance principles.  
2. Non-compliance may result in suspension or termination of the partnership.

---

## Article 6 — Duration and Termination

1. Partnership arrangements are established for a defined period.  
2. Either party may terminate cooperation in accordance with agreed terms.

---

## Article 7 — Entry into Force

This Partnership Framework Agreement template enters into force upon its official publication as part of the ECRP Governance Framework.
